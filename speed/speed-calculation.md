# Gross speed
WPM (word per minute)
<br>
Taking a [a relative link](speed/sample-text1.md) seems average letters per word on a paragraph (including space) is 6
<br>
So, our formula for gross speed is : 
<br>
Gross WPM = (All typed Entries/6)/Time
